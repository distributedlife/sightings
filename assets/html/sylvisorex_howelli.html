Howell's forest shrew

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Howell's forest shrew (Sylvisorex howelli) is a species of mammal in the Soricidae family. It is endemic to Tanzania. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests.

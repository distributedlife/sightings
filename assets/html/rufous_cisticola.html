Rufous cisticola

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Rufous Cisticola (Cisticola rufus) is a species of bird in the Cisticolidae family. It is found in Burkina Faso, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Chad, Gambia, Ghana, Guinea, Mali, Nigeria, Senegal, Sierra Leone, and Togo. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry shrubland and subtropical or tropical dry lowland grassland.

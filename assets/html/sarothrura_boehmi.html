Streaky-breasted flufftail

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Streaky-breasted Flufftail (Sarothrura boehmi) is a species of bird in the Sarothruridae family.

It is found in Angola, Burundi, Cameroon, Republic of the Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Guinea, Kenya, Malawi, Mali, Nigeria, Rwanda, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

The name of this bird commemorates the German zoologist Richard Böhm.

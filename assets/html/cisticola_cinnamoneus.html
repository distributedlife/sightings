Pale-crowned cisticola

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Pale-crowned Cisticola (Cisticola cinnemoneus) is a species of bird in the Cisticolidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Republic of the Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Eritrea, Gabon, Mozambique, South Africa, Tanzania, Zambia and Zimbabwe. Its natural habitats are damp or marshy areas in upland grassland.

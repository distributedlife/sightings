Chirping cisticola

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Chirping Cisticola (Cisticola pipiens) is a species of bird in the Cisticolidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Burundi, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Namibia, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical seasonally wet or flooded lowland grassland and swamps.

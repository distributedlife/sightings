Spike-heeled lark

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Spike-heeled Lark (Chersomanes albofasciata) is a species of lark in the Alaudidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Lesotho, Namibia, and South Africa.

Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry shrubland and subtropical or tropical seasonally wet or flooded lowland grassland.

Grey apalis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Grey Apalis (Apalis cinerea) is a species of bird in the Cisticolidae family.

It is found in Angola, Burundi, Cameroon, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Kenya, Malawi, Nigeria, Rwanda, Sudan, Tanzania, Uganda, and Zambia. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry forests and subtropical or tropical moist montane forests.

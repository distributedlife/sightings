Red-winged lark

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Red-winged Lark (Mirafra hypermetra) is a species of lark in the Alaudidae family.

Population and range

The range of Red-winged Lark is quite extensive within Ethiopia, Kenya, Somalia, Sudan, Tanzania, and Uganda, with an estimated global Extent of Occurrence of 660,000 km.

Its total population has yet to be quantified, but is believed to be large.

Habitat

Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical dry lowland grassland.

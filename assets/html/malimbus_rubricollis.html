Red-headed malimbe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Red-headed Malimbe (Malimbus rubricollis) is a species of bird in the Ploceidae family. It is found in Angola, Benin, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Republic of the Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ivory Coast, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Ghana, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Kenya, Liberia, Mali, Nigeria, Sierra Leone, Sudan, Tanzania, Togo, and Uganda.

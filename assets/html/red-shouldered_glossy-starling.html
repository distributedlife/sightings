Cape starling

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Cape starling, red-shouldered glossy-starling or Cape glossy starling (Lamprotornis nitens) is a species of starling in the Sturnidae family.

It is found in Angola, Botswana, the Republic of the Congo, Gabon, Lesotho, Mozambique, Namibia, South Africa, Swaziland, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

Cape Starling in Etosha National Park, Namibia

It grows to about 22 cm in size.

Its habitat is woodlands, bushveld and in suburbs.

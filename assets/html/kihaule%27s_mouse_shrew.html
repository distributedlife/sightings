Kihaule's mouse shrew

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kihaule's mouse shrew (Myosorex kihaulei) is a species of mammal in the Soricidae family. It is endemic to Tanzania. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montane forests and plantations . It is threatened by habitat loss.

White-bellied storm petrel

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The white-bellied storm petrel (Fregetta grallaria) is a species of seabird in the Hydrobatidae family.

It is found in Angola, Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Chile, Ecuador, French Polynesia, French Southern Territories, Maldives, Namibia, New Zealand, Saint Helena, South Africa, and South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands.

Its natural habitat is open seas.

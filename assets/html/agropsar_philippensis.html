Chestnut-cheeked starling

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The chestnut-cheeked starling (Agropsar philippensis) is a species of starling in the Sturnidae family. It is sometimes placed in the genus Sturnia or Sturnus. It is found in Brunei, China, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Japan, North Korea, South Korea, Malaysia, the Philippines, Russia, Singapore, and Taiwan. Its natural habitat is temperate forests.

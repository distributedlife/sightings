Chestnut-winged starling

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The chestnut-winged starling (Onychognathus fulgidus) is a species of starling in the Sturnidae family. It is found in Angola, Benin, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Republic of the Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ivory Coast, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Ghana, Guinea, Liberia, Nigeria, São Tomé and Príncipe, Sierra Leone, South Sudan Sudan, Tanzania, Togo, and Uganda. It also found in Kolkata India

Giant roundleaf bat

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Giant roundleaf bat, Hipposideros gigas, is a species of bat in the family Hipposideridae found in Angola, Benin, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, the Central African Republic, the Republic of the Congo, the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ivory Coast, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Ghana, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Kenya, Mali, Nigeria, Senegal, Sierra Leone, Tanzania, and Togo. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests and caves. It is threatened by habitat loss.

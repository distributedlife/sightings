The African grey flycatcher or large flycatcher (Bradornis microrhynchus) is a species of bird in the Muscicapidae family.

Taxonomy

The subspecies B. m. pumilus is sometimes considered a full species, the Ethiopian grey flycatcher (Bradornis pumilus).

Distribution and habitat

It is found in Ethiopia, Kenya, Somalia, Sudan, Tanzania, and Uganda. Its natural habitats are dry savanna and subtropical or tropical dry shrubland.

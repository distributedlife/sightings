Tullberg's woodpecker

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tullberg's Woodpecker (Campethera tullbergi) is a species of bird in the Picidae family. It is found in Burundi, Cameroon, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Equatorial Guinea, Kenya, Nigeria, Rwanda, Tanzania, and Uganda.

The common name and Latin binomial commemorates the Swedish zoologist Tycho Fredirk Hugo Tullberg (1842-1920).

Grant's rock mouse

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Grant's Rock Mouse or Grant's Rock Rat (Aethomys granti) is a species of rodent in the family Muridae. It is found only in South Africa. It is sometimes included in the genus Micaelamys. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry shrubland and rocky areas.

Yellow-throated petronia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Yellow-throated Petronia (Petronia superciliaris), also known as the Yellow-throated Sparrow, is a species of bird in the sparrow family Passeridae. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Burundi, Republic of the Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Gabon, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia, South Africa, Swaziland, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry forests, dry savanna, and subtropical or tropical dry shrubland.

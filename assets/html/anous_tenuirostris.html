Lesser noddy

The lesser noddy (Anous tenuirostris), also known as the sooty noddy, is a species of tern in the Sternidae family. It is found near the coastlines of Comoros, Kenya, Liberia, India, Maldives, Mauritius, Seychelles, South Africa, Sri Lanka and United Arab Emirates.

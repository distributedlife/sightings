Green-throated sunbird

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Green-throated Sunbird (Chalcomitra rubescens) is a species of bird in the Nectariniidae family. It is found in Angola, Burundi, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Republic of the Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Kenya, Nigeria, Rwanda, Sudan, Tanzania, Uganda, and Zambia.

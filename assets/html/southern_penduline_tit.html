Cape penduline tit

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Cape penduline tit or southern penduline tit (Anthoscopus minutus) is a species of bird in the Remizidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Namibia, South Africa, and Zimbabwe. Its natural habitats are dry savannah, subtropical or tropical dry shrubland, and Mediterranean-type shrubby vegetation. At 8 cm (3.1 in) in length, it is one of the smallest species of bird found in Africa, along with its cousins the grey penduline tit and the mouse-coloured penduline tit.

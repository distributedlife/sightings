Buff-spotted flufftail

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
specimen at Nairobi National Museum

The Buff-spotted Flufftail (Sarothrura elegans) is a species of bird in the Sarothruridae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Burundi, Cameroon, Republic of the Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ivory Coast, Equatorial Guinea, Ethiopia, Gabon, Guinea, Kenya, Liberia, Malawi, Mozambique, Nigeria, Rwanda, Sierra Leone, Somalia, South Africa, South Sudan, Swaziland, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

Singing bush lark

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Not to be confused with Horsfield's Bush Lark (Mirafra javanica) of Australia and Southeast Asia, which is also called the Singing Bush Lark.

The Singing Bush Lark (Mirafra cantillans) is a species of lark found in Africa, the Middle East, and South Asia.

Range and population

The range of M. cantillans is quite extensive, with an estimated global Extent of Occurrence of 10,000,000 km; and its global population is thought to be large, though it has yet to be quantified.

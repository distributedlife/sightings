Hipposideros marungensis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hipposideros marungensis is a species of bat in the family Hipposideridae. It is found in Botswana, Central African Republic, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ethiopia, Kenya, Mozambique, Namibia, Somalia, Tanzania, Zambia, Zimbabwe, and possibly Guinea-Bissau. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, dry savanna, and caves. It is threatened by habitat loss.

Cape long-billed lark

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Cape long-billed lark (Certhilauda curvirostris), also known as the Cape lark is a species of lark in the Alaudidae family. It is found in South Africa. Its natural habitats are semi-arid Karoo shrub and subtropical dry shrubland and subtropical or dry lowland and highveld grassland. This lark is also found in croplands, farmlands and coastal fynbos.

Buff-crested bustard

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Buff-crested Bustard (Lophotis gindiana) is a species of bird in the Otididae family. It is found in Djibouti, Ethiopia, Kenya, Somalia, Sudan, Tanzania, and Uganda.

Banded parisoma

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Banded Parisoma, Banded Tit Warbler, or Banded Warbler (Sylvia boehmi) is a species of Old World warbler in the Sylviidae family. It is found in Ethiopia, Kenya, Somalia, and Tanzania. Its natural habitat is dry savanna.

Length: 4.75 in (12.1 cm)

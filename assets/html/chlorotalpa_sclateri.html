Sclater's golden mole

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sclater's golden mole (Chlorotalpa sclateri) is a species of mammal in the Chrysochloridae family. It is found in Lesotho and South Africa. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry shrubland, subtropical or tropical high-altitude shrubland, Mediterranean-type shrubby vegetation, temperate grassland, arable land, pastureland, rural gardens, and urban areas.

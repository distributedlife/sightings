Northern brown-throated weaver

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Northern Brown-throated Weaver (Ploceus castanops) is a species of bird in the Ploceidae family. It is found in Burundi, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Kenya, Rwanda, Tanzania, and Uganda.

Hybridisation

P. victoriae Ash, 1986 is now thought to be a hybrid between P. castanops and P. melanocephalus.

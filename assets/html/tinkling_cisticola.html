Tinkling cisticola

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
For a bird alternatively named tinkling cisticola, Cisticola tinniens, see Levaillant's cisticola.

The tinkling cisticola or grey cisticola (Cisticola rufilatus) is a species of bird in the Cisticolidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Republic of the Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Gabon, Malawi, Namibia, South Africa, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. Its natural habitat is dry savannah.

Note that the name tinkling cisticola is also used as an alternative name for Levaillant's cisticola (Cisticola tinniens).

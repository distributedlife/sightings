Cloud cisticola

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Cloud Cisticola or Tink-tink Cisticola (Cisticola textrix) is a species of bird in the Cisticolidae family. It is found in Angola, Lesotho, Mozambique, South Africa, Swaziland, and Zambia, and its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical dry lowland grassland.

Olive woodpecker

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Olive Woodpecker (Mesopicos griseocephalus) is a species of bird in the Picidae family. It is found in Angola, Burundi, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia, Rwanda, South Africa, Swaziland, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

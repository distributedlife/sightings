Yellow-bellied waxbill

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Yellow-bellied Waxbill (Coccopygia quartinia) is species of estrildid finch found in Sub-Saharan Africa.

It breeds in east central and southeastern Africa. Some taxonomists consider it to be conscpecific with the Swee Waxbill.

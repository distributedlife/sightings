Forest tube-nosed bat

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Forest Tube-Nosed Bat (Murina silvatica) is a species of vesper bat in the Vespertilionidae family. It is found only in Japan, where it is called the Japanese Kotengu Bat (ニホンコテングコウモリ, Nihon Kotengu Kōmori).

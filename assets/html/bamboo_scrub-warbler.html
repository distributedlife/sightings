Bamboo warbler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bamboo Warbler or Bamboo Scrub-warbler (Bradypterus alfredi) is a species of Old World warbler in the Locustellidae family. It is found in Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ethiopia, Sudan, Tanzania, Uganda, and Zambia. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montane forests and subtropical or tropical moist shrubland.

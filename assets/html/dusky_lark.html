Dusky lark

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Dusky Lark (Pinarocorys nigricans) is a species of lark in the Alaudidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia, South Africa, Swaziland, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. Its natural habitats are dry savanna and subtropical or tropical dry lowland grassland.

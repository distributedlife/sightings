Short-toed rock thrush

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The short-toed rock thrush (Monticola brevipes) is a species of bird in the Muscicapidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Namibia, and South Africa. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical dry shrubland.

Birds in the eastern part of its range are sometimes regarded as a separate species, the Pretoria rock thrush or Transvaal rock thrush (M. pretoriae).

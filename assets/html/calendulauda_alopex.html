Foxy lark

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Foxy Lark or Abyssinian Lark (Calendulauda alopex) is a species of lark in the Alaudidae family. It is found in eastern Ethiopia and northwest Somalia.

Range, population and habitat

The Foxy Lark has a large range, with an estimated global Extent of Occurrence of 100,000-1,000,000 square km. and is found in dense, thorny scrubland.

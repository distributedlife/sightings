Sakeji horseshoe bat

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Sakeji horseshoe bat (Rhinolophus sakejiensis) is a species of bat in the Rhinolophidae family. It is endemic to Zambia. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry forests, subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, and moist savanna, especially Zambia, Mwinilunga District, Ikelenge Pedicle between the Sakeji and Zambezi rivers, approximately 11 km NNE of source of Zambezi River, Kavunda, 11°17'S, 24°21'E, 1,388 m. It is threatened by habitat loss. It was discovered in 2000

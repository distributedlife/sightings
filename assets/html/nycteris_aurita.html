Andersen's slit-faced bat

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Geographic Range

This species is found in various locations in East Africa. It has been found recorded in Northern Somalia, through Sudan and Ethiopia, into Kenya and Tanzania.

Countries

Zambia, Uganda, Tanzania, Somalia, Kenya, Ethiopia.

Population

there are no information recorded on the exact population number of this species.

Habitat and Ecology

this species is connected with the savanna habitats. Also, it appears this species found in semi desert.

Threats

There are no records regarding the major threats.

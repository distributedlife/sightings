Spotted palm thrush

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Spotted Palm Thrush (Cichladusa guttata), also known as the Spotted Morning-thrush, is a species of bird in the Muscicapidae family. It is found in Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ethiopia, Kenya, Somalia, Sudan, Tanzania, and Uganda. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry forests, dry savanna, and subtropical or tropical moist shrubland.

Hinde's lesser house bat

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hinde's lesser house bat (Scotoecus hindei) is a species of vesper bat. It is found in Cameroon, Republic of the Congo, Kenya, Malawi, Mozambique, Nigeria, Somalia, Sudan, Tanzania, and Zambia. Its natural habitat is dry savanna.

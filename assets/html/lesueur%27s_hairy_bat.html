Lesueur's hairy bat

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lesueur's Hairy Bat (Cistugo lesueuri) also known as Lesueur's Wing-gland Bat is a species of bat in the Cistugidae family. It is found in dry savanna, Mediterranean-type shrubby vegetation, and hot deserts in Lesotho and South Africa. It is threatened by habitat loss.

Meves's starling

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Meves's starling or Meves's glossy-starling (Lamprotornis mevesii) is a species of starling in the Sturnidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia, South Africa, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

Its common name and Latin binomial commemorate the German ornithologist Friederich Wilhelm Meves.

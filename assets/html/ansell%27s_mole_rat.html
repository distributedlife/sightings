Ansell's mole rat

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ansell's mole rat (Fukomys anselli) is a species of rodent in the family Bathyergidae. It is endemic to Zambia. Its natural habitats are moist savanna and miombo forests. It is noted for its very long tunnels, up to 2.8 km for a single colony of only ten individuals.

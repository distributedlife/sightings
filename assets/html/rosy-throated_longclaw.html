Rosy-throated longclaw

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Rosy-throated Longclaw, also known as the Rosy-breasted Longclaw (Macronyx ameliae) is a species of bird in the Motacillidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Kenya, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia, South Africa, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical seasonally wet or flooded lowland grassland.

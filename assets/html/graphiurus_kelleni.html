Kellen's dormouse

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kellen's dormouse (Graphiurus kelleni) is a species of rodent in the Gliridae family.

Distribution

It is found in Angola, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Gambia, Kenya, Mali, Nigeria, Niger, Senegal, Tanzania, and Uganda.

Habitat

Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical, dry forests and moist and dry savanna.

Chinese grosbeak

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Chinese grosbeak or yellow-billed grosbeak (Eophona migratoria) is a species of finch in the Fringillidae family.

It can be found in temperate forests in China, Hong Kong, Japan, North and South Korea, Laos, Myanmar, Russia, Taiwan, Thailand, and Vietnam.

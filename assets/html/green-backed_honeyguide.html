Green-backed honeybird

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Green-backed Honeybird (Prodotiscus zambesiae), also known as the Eastern Green-backed Honeyguide, Green-backed Honeyguide and Slender-billed Honeyguide, is a species of bird in the Indicatoridae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Kenya, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

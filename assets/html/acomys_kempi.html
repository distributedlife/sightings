Kemp's spiny mouse

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kemp's Spiny Mouse (Acomys kempi) is a species of rodent in the family Muridae. It is found in Kenya, Somalia, and Tanzania. Its natural habitats are dry savanna and rocky areas. It is one of only two mammals, the other being Acomys percivali, known to be capable of completely regenerating damaged tissue, including hair follicles, skin, sweat glands, fur and cartilage.

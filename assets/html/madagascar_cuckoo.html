Madagascan cuckoo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Madagascan Cuckoo (Cuculus rochii), also known as the Madagascar Lesser Cuckoo, is a species of cuckoo in the Cuculidae family. Though it breeds only in Madagascar, it spends the non-breeding season in a number of countries in the African Great Lakes region and the Indian Ocean islands: Burundi, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Madagascar, Malawi, Rwanda, South Africa, Uganda and Zambia.

Description

The Madagascan Cuckoo is a small, slim cuckoo, measuring 28 cm (11 in) in length.

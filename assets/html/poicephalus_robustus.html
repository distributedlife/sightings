Poicephalus fuscicollis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Poicephalus fuscicollis is a large Poicephalus parrot species endemic to Africa consisting of the savanna-dwelling Brown-necked Parrot (P. fuscicollis fuscicollis) and Grey-headed Parrot (P. f. suahelicus) subspecies. It formerly included the Cape Parrot (now Poicephalus robustus) as a subspecies before the Cape Parrot was re-classified as a distinct species.

Japanese red-backed vole

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Japanese Red-backed Vole or Wakayama Red-backed Vole or Anderson's Red-backed Vole (Myodes andersoni) is a species of rodent in the family Cricetidae. It is found only in Japan.

Ryukyu robin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Ryukyu robin (Erithacus komadori) is a bird endemic to the Ryūkyū Islands, of Japan. It is sometimes placed in the genus Luscinia, but recent research suggests that the East Asian robins belong in a new genus uniting them with some East Asian Luscinia members such as the Siberian blue robin.

The specific name komadori is, somewhat confusingly, the common name of its relative the Japanese robin in Japanese.

Miombo wren-warbler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The miombo wren-warbler (Calamonastes undosus), also known as the miombo barred warbler or pale wren-warbler, is a species of bird in the Cisticolidae family found in southern Africa. It is sometimes placed in the genus Calamonastes.

Stierling's wren-warbler, Caamonastes (undosa) stierlingi, is often included in this species.

Ashy starling

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The ashy starling (Lamprotornis unicolor) is a species of starling in the Sturnidae family. It is found in Kenya and Tanzania. It is alternatively placed in the genus Cosmopsarus or Spreo.

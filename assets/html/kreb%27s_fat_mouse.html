Krebs's fat mouse

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Krebs's Fat Mouse (Steatomys krebsii) is a species of rodent in the Nesomyidae family. It is found in Angola, Botswana, Lesotho, Namibia, South Africa, and Zambia. Its natural habitats are Mediterranean-type shrubby vegetation and subtropical or tropical high-altitude grassland. Its name honours Georg Ludwig Engelhard Krebs (1792-1844), a German natural history collector in South Africa.

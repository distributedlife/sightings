Reichenow's seedeater

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Reichenow's seedeater (Serinus reichenowi), is a species of finch in the Fringillidae family. It is sometimes considered conspecific with the black-throated canary.

Distribution

It is found frequently in eastern Africa. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry forests, dry savanna, and subtropical or tropical dry shrubland.

Baglafecht weaver

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Photographed at Bwindi NP, Uganda

The Baglafecht Weaver (Ploceus baglafecht) is a species of bird in the Ploceidae family. It is found in Burundi, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Eritrea, Ethiopia, Kenya, Malawi, Nigeria, Rwanda, Sudan, Tanzania, Uganda, and Zambia. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests and subtropical or tropical moist montane forests.

Scaly chatterer

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Scaly Chatterer (Turdoides aylmeri) is a species of bird in the Leiothrichidae family. It is also known as the Bare-eyed Babbler. It is found in Ethiopia, Kenya, Somalia, and Tanzania. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical dry shrubland.

Prigogine's double-collared sunbird

The Prigogine's Double-collared Sunbird (Cinnyris prigoginei) is a species of bird in the Nectariniidae family. It is found in east central Africa. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical dry forests. It is threatened by habitat loss. It is sometimes considered a subspecies of the Greater Double-collared Sunbird.

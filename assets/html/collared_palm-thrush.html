Collared palm thrush

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The collared palm thrush (Cichladusa arquata) is a species of bird in the Muscicapidae family. It is found in Botswana, Burundi, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Kenya, Malawi, Mozambique, Rwanda, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. Its natural habitats are dry savannah and subtropical or tropical moist shrubland.
